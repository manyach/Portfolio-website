About Me

Dear Music Enthusiasts, Allow me to share a melody-woven glimpse of my world with you. I am Bhawana Awasthi, a bearer of musical stories that have resonated not only across Delhi but also far beyond, leaving an indelible imprint on the hearts of diverse audiences. As the proud founder of Nav Kala Bhaw Sangam, I have the privilege of orchestrating performances that interlace narratives and emotions into mesmerizing spectacles. Each chord struck, each lyric sung, weaves a narrative that stays with you long after the last note fades. I invite you to remain connected with me. Let us keep the harmonies alive and the melodies reverberating in the chambers of our souls.

Music Timeline

Started at the age of four and been in a number of stage shows, as well as being a graded artist on "All India Radio".

Stepped into the Delhi Music Faternity in 2017, performed various live stage shows and quickly rose to fame. In 2018, I started my own musical society "Nav Kala Bhaw Sangam".

Apart from Singing I have also given my voice to many Devotional songs, garnering applause for the same.
